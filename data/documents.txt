How to Roll a Perfect Burrito 🌯
1. Start with the right setup
Use a large, soft tortilla (warm it slightly so it doesn’t crack).
Place your fillings in a horizontal line just below the center.

Don’t overfill — that’s the #1 mistake. Think:
- Protein (chicken, beef, beans)
- Rice or base
- Veggies
- Sauce (not too watery)
2. Fold the bottom up
Lift the bottom edge of the tortilla and fold it up over the filling, tucking it snugly underneath.

This creates a seal so everything doesn’t fall out the bottom.
3. Fold in the sides
Fold the left and right sides inward toward the center.

Keep it tight—this is what gives that neat, compact burrito shape.
4. Roll it up tightly
Roll the burrito forward away from you, keeping pressure so it stays compact.

Think: tuck → squeeze → roll
5. Optional: Toast it
Place seam-side down on a hot pan for 1–2 minutes.

- Seals the burrito
- Adds structure
- Enhances flavor

Pro Tips
- Warm tortillas = no tearing
- Keep sauces minimal or thick
- Distribute filling evenly
- Use your fingers to pull filling back as you roll for tightness
